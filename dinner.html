Veggies & quinoa

Serves 2
    Ingredients:
  • Quinoa
  • Carrots
  • Broccoli
  • Zucchini
  • Red pepper
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Brussel sprouts (optional)
    Recipe:
  1. Cook the quinoa according to package directions.
  2. Add some water in a wok pan and bring it to a boil.
  3. Then add the veggies making sure they’re submerged in water and cook for 1 to 2 minutes over high-heat. Drain the veggies and set aside.
  4. Heat the oil in the wok and add the onion and garlic. When they’re golden cook the veggies on high-heat for about 1 minute, stirring occasionally.
  5. Add the quinoa and seasoning of your choice. Heat through.
