Breakfast oats

Serves 1
    Ingredients:
  • 1/2 cup of oats (any brand)
  • 1/2 cup of plant milk
  • 1 tsp of hempseeds or chia seeds
  • 1 cup fresh/frozen berries (strawberries, raspberries, blueberries)
    The night before:
  1. Add your oats into a bowl or container
  2. Pour in the plant milk
  3. Using a blender, blend your berries until smooth and layer it on top
  4. Sprinkle on the hempseeds/chia seeds
  5. Refrigerate overnight
    6. In the morning:
  6. Add your oats into a bowl or container
  7. Pour in the plant milk
  8. Microwave for 2 minutes
  9. Add fresh berries and seeds
