Sweet potato & kale bowl

Serves 2
    Ingredients:
  • cup of edamame beans
  • 1 large sweet potato
  • 1 bag of kale
  • half a cup of quinoa
  • half a cup of rice
  • tumeric
  • salt
  • black pepper
  • tomato sauce
  • 1 onion
  • corianer leaves
  • bell pepper
for kale marinade
  • 3 tbsp of soy sauce
  • 3 tbsp of olive oil
  • 3 tbsp of lemon juice
  • 1 tbsp of onion powder
  1. wash the edamame beans and boil for 30mins
  2. wash the sweet potato then peel and chop into cubes
  3. steam the potato for 20 mins
  4. wash the quinoa until the wash runs clear and boil with 1 tsp of tumeric for 15mins
  5. wash the rice then add half a cup of water, 1 tsp of salt and oil
  6. cook the rice; once the water boils move to low heat for 7mins
  7. wash the kale and add the marinade to it
  8. massage the kale to wilt it for 5-7 mins
  9. chop the onion and fry in oil until brown
  10. add half a cup of tomato sauce, 2 tsp of salt, 1 tsp of black pepper and 1 tsp of tumeric
  11. simmer the sauce for 5 mins
  12. plate the cooked ingredients
  13. garnish with diced bell pepper,sliced avocado and corianer leaves
